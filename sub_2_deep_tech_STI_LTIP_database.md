## STI & LTIP COMPENSATION TRENDS (2024-2025)

### Short-Term Incentive (STI) Characteristics:

**Typical Structure:**
- **Target:** 50-150% of base salary
- **Metrics:** Revenue (40%), EBITDA (30%), Strategic milestones (30%)
- **Payout Range:** 0-200% of target
- **2024 Median Payout:** 118% of target for energy sector CEOs

**Micro-Cap Reality (<$300M market cap):**
- Base salaries: $200-400K
- STI often represents 20-40% of total compensation
- Many sub-$1 companies skip formal STI in favor of pure equity

### Long-Term Incentive Plan (LTIP) Characteristics:

**Typical Structure:**
- **Vehicles:** Stock options (50%), RSUs (30%), PSUs (20%)
- **Vesting:** 3-4 years with 1-year cliff
- **Performance Metrics:**
  - Market Cap Milestones (most common for micro-caps)
  - TSR (Total Shareholder Return)
  - Revenue/Profitability Targets
  - Product Launch Milestones
  - Strategic Partnerships

**Micro-Cap Reality:**
- **Equity % of Total Comp:** 40-94% (median ~65%)
- **Founder CEO Ownership:** 15-75%+
- **Option Grants:** Often 0.25-2% of outstanding shares
- **Moonshot Potential:** NO companies found with explicit 200x targets

**Key Finding:**
The smaller the market cap, the more extreme the equity ratio:
- **<$50M:** 70-94% equity
- **$50-$150M:** 60-80% equity
- **$150-$300M:** 40-60% equity

## HOW TO ACCESS FULL PROXY DATA

### SEC EDGAR Database:
1. Visit: https://www.sec.gov/edgar/searchedgar/companysearch.html
2. Search by ticker symbol
3. Filter for "DEF 14A" filings
4. Look for:
   - **Summary Compensation Table**
   - **Grants of Plan-Based Awards Table**
   - **Compensation Discussion & Analysis (CD&A)**
   - **Outstanding Equity Awards Table**

### Key Sections for STI & LTIP:

**For STI:**
- "Annual Incentive Plan" or "Short-Term Incentive"
- "Non-Equity Incentive Plan Compensation" column
- Performance metrics description in CD&A

**For LTIP:**
- "Stock Awards" and "Option Awards" columns
- "Grants of Plan-Based Awards" table
- Vesting schedules in footnotes
- Performance conditions in CD&A
