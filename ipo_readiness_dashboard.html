Methodology & Limitations

Data Source

Deep Tech 2026 Private Company Database containing 95,247 active companies (US 68%, Canada 21%, UK 8%, Other 3%). Financial data from FY2023-2024. Analysis conducted December 2025.

Scoring Framework

Seven-dimension model weighted by empirical importance from IPO success literature:

  • Financial (30%): Revenue thresholds, profitability, R&D intensity
  • Governance (25%): Board independence, committee structure, expertise
  • Legal/Compliance (15%): Litigation exposure, regulatory enforcement
  • IP/Innovation (10%): Patent portfolio strength, citation quality
  • Risk (10%): Cybersecurity, customer concentration, key person risk
  • Operational (10%): Certifications (SOC2, ISO), executive stability

Limitations

  • Data Completeness: 47% of companies lack complete FY2024 data; used FY2023 where unavailable
  • Governance Opacity: Board composition directly observable for only 34% of companies; 41% inferred from LinkedIn/bios
  • Geographic Skew: Heavy North American focus (89%) may not generalize to European/Asian markets
  • Sector Concentration: 76% technology/biotech; traditional industries underrepresented
  • Point-in-Time: Snapshot analysis; IPO readiness changes dynamically with company growth
  • Linear Scoring: Uses additive weights; actual IPO success may have non-linear interactions
