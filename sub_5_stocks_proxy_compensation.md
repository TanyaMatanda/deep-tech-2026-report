## Key Insights from Proxy Data

### Compensation Structure Patterns:

1. **Micro-caps (<$100M) favor extreme equity ratios**
   - DTSS: 94% equity
   - KSCP: 77% stock options/bonuses
   - ARBE: 60% equity

2. **Founder CEOs have massive skin in the game**
   - SIDU: Founder CEO Carol Craig
   - KSCP: Founder CEO owns >15%

3. **Performance-based vesting is standard**
   - 3-4 year vesting schedules
   - RSUs tied to continued service
   - Bonuses tied to revenue/profitability milestones

4. **Stock option grants are substantial relative to market cap**
   - KSCP: $1.2M options on $275M market cap (0.44%)
   - DTSS: $973K equity on $40M market cap (2.4%)

### Motivation Analysis:

**Top 3 Most Motivated CEOs (by compensation structure):**
1. **DTSS (Datasea)** - 94% equity compensation
2. **SIDU (Sidus Space)** - Founder CEO + NASA contracts  
3. **KSCP (Knightscope)** - $1.2M stock options + >15% founder ownership

## Accessing Full Proxy Circulars

All DEF 14A filings available at:
- **SEC EDGAR:** https://www.sec.gov/edgar/searchedgar/companysearch.html
- **Company Investor Relations:** Each company's website under "Investors" section

**Search Tips:**
1. Search company ticker on SEC.gov
2. Filter by "DEF 14A" (Definitive Proxy Statement)
3. Look for "Summary Compensation Table" section
4. Review "Grants of Plan-Based Awards" table
