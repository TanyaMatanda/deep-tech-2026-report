# State of Women on Boards - 2025 Proxy Season Analysis

> **Note:** This analysis is based on the Deep Tech company dataset (N=500).

## Methodology

- **Dataset:** 500 Deep Tech companies (Public & Private).
- **Data Source:** `board_composition_annual` table from the Vendor Governance database.
- **Fiscal Year:** 2025 (representing Proxy Season 2025 / FY 2024 financials).
- **Metric:** Percentage of women directors per board.

## Executive Summary

- **Average Women on Boards (2025):** 11.4%
- **Average Women on Boards (2024):** 11.5%
- **Year-over-Year Change:** -0.1%
